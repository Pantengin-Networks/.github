# Pantengin Networks

Pantengin Networks is a growing open-source learning community for people who want to learn, build, and share practical knowledge.

We focus on practical learning around:

- AI
- Coding
- Data
- Digital Marketing
- Technology experiments
- Other useful topics for builders

This community is open for beginners, solo developers, vibe coders, CTOs, and anyone who wants to keep learning by building.

## Everyone Is Welcome

Pantengin Networks is still growing.

You are welcome to explore the repositories, learn from the resources, contribute improvements, and share ideas with the community.
